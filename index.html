WHO WE ARE.?

Actually,we are the Main Medical Center of the University of Ruhuna. The main medical centre is located in a building close to the Department of Physics.We provides preventive and curative care to students, staff and their family members.
During the Acedamic year we are opening in week days, from 8.00 a.m. to 4.00 p.m. to treat students and staff.We provide Drugs and our service free of charge for students.

There are Dental Clinical Unit established in the medical centre and the Ayurvedic Medical Treatment Center which is located in the upper floor of the Peoples Bank,Wellamadama branch.
