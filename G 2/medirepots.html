Medical Reports

  • As the main medical center of the University of Ruhuna, we can verifty medical reports for your identified diseases. Considering your medical reasons,we can accept your medical report requests for absence lectures and exams.

Giving Medical for Exams

We are verifing medicals for Exam which you can not to participate because of the acceptable medical reasons.If you unable to sit for exam,you should follow the below steps.
If a student is unable to attend for the lectures or practical classes because of the illness,student should follow the below steps.

  1. Immediately inform to the university medical officer and the examination branch.
  2. If your medical certificates obtained from outside medical officers,
    • It should be a certificate issued by the government hospital,
    • It should be a certificate issued by the specialist doctor or
    • It should be a certificate issued by private hospital for your residential medicine.
  3. Your medical request should be under the 14 days and other private medical reports will be rejected.

Giving Medical for Absence Lectures

  1. You should inform to the university medical officer within a week.
  2. If you submit the medical report through the medical center,you have to take the medicine from the madical center.
  3. If you expect to submit the private medical report,
    • It should be a certificate issued by the government hospital or
    • It should be a certificate issued by the private doctor.
  4. This reports is only available for a maximum of 5 days.
All these facts also valid for the Aurvadic Medical Center of the University.
